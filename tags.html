Kolobok

Kolobok is a fairy tale familiar to every adult and will also appeal to small children. They quickly learn the simple words of Kolobok's song and happily sing along with their parents. From this fairy tale, kids will learn how grandma baked Kolobok for grandpa and put him on the window to cool. Kolobok got tired of lying there and decided to jump off the window and roll out of the yard and out of the gate. On the way he met the Hare, the Wolf and the Bear, sang them his song and ran away from them, the animals could not eat him. Kolobok rolled on and met the Fox. The Kolobok tried to deceive her, but he failed, the Fox turned out to be more cunning. She lured him closer and ate the tasty Kolobok.
